PIN APPLICATION

PIN registration is an online process done on iTax.

Residents receive their PIN Certificate instantly after completing the online registration form.

Requirements

  • National ID/Alien ID Card details.
  • Employers' PIN details for those who are employed.
  • Business registration certificate details for those in business.

How to:

  1. Visit KRA iTax Portal using itax.kra.go.ke/KRA-Portal and select "New PIN Registration.

    2. Select the Taxpayer Type (Individual or Non-individual) and Mode of Registration (Online or Upload form) in e-Registration form.

  2. Based on the taxpayer type and registration mode, iTax PIN registration form will open. The type of details to be furnished in the PIN registration form are different for individual and Non-individual.

    3. For Non-individuals, the PIN registration form consists of 4 broad categories listed below:

    • Basic Information
    • Obligation
    • Director Associates
    • Agent Details

  3. You need to furnish all the mandatory details along with the required supporting documents and submit the KRA iTax PIN registration form.
  4. On submitting, a pop-up to confirm the e-mail ID you had mentioned in the PIN registration form. You can confirm by clicking ‘OKAY’ if the ID is correct.
  5. On successful submission of the iTAX PIN registration form, iTAX PIN will be displayed immediately on the iTax page. You can also download it and print. Another copy of your iTAX PIN will be mailed to your Email.
  6. Updating your iTax Login credentials.
    Login to your email and you will find your iTax PIN and password generated by KRA iTax Portal upon succesfully registering for a PIN. Visit KRA iTax Portal and key in the iTax PIN and password you have received. You will be then re-directed to password change page. Here, you can change the password and also to create a security question which will help you in recovering your iTax profile.
