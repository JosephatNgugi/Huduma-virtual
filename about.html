About Us

What We Do

Huduma Virtual is an information hub for everyone trying to access a government of Kenya service and has no idea where to start. We provide detailed information and guides that can help the user navigate through the procedures with ease.

We also provide our users with information regarding the documents and requirements that they will need before hand to ensure preparedness. As the saying goes, “By failing to to prepare, you are preparing to fail.”

Government service processes are know to be long and frustrating to many Kenyans. We hope through equipping users with the right information, it makes it all a smooth-sailing affair. Through guides, requirements and tutorials provided on the website,users can easily follow the steps and direct links to ease the frustration.

Our goal is to ensure that our website is helpful to Kenyans and anyone else in need of a government service by providing the ultimate guide that makes the process easier and untroubled.
